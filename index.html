TiFDIZZYMALL

A tiny mall-run homage built from the loading screen vibe.

Score: 0
Shops Cleared: 0/4
Time: 90

How to play

  • Move with ← → or A D
  • Jump with ↑, W or Space
  • On iPhone, use the touch buttons below the game
  • Ride escalators to reach the mall shops
  • Collect the 4 shop tokens, then reach the directory

Clear the mall before time runs out.
