TiFDIZZYMALL

Explore the mall, meet shoppers, collect and trade items, and follow Dizzy-style objectives at your own pace.

Screen: Atrium
Inventory: 0
Objectives done: 0/0
🕹️ Joystick

How to play

  • Move with ← → or A D
  • Jump with ↑, W or Space
  • Press T / 💬 TALK near characters
  • Press E / 🤏 TAKE near items
  • Press G / 🎁 GIVE near a character to hand over the needed item
  • No timer, no ending — just explore the 10-screen mall and clear objectives when you feel like it

Inventory

  • Empty pockets.

Objectives

  • Talk to the mall visitors.

Walk around the atrium and press TALK when you meet someone.
